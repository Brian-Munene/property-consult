Kigali, Rwanda

The City of a Thousand Hills

Kigali, the capital and largest city of Rwanda, is known for its cleanliness, safety, and stunning hilly landscape. The city has undergone remarkable transformation since the 1994 genocide, emerging as one of Africa's most modern and well-organized cities. Kigali is a hub for business, culture, and tourism, offering a unique blend of traditional Rwandan culture and modern development.
