Addis Ababa

Addis Ababa, the capital of Ethiopia, is a vibrant and rapidly growing city that serves as the political and economic hub of the country. Known as the "New Flower," Addis Ababa is a city of contrasts, where modern skyscrapers stand alongside traditional markets and historical landmarks.

The city's real estate market is booming, with new developments in areas like Bole, Kazanchis, and CMC. Addis Ababa's strategic location as the diplomatic capital of Africa, combined with its growing economy, makes it an attractive destination for property investment.

Addis Ababa offers a unique blend of cultural heritage and modern development, with numerous museums, historical sites, and modern amenities. The city's elevation of 2,355 meters above sea level gives it a pleasant climate year-round, making it an ideal place to live and work.
