Nairobi, Kenya

Nairobi, Kenya's capital and largest city, is a vibrant metropolis that serves as the economic and cultural hub of East Africa. Known as the "Green City in the Sun," Nairobi offers a unique blend of urban sophistication and natural beauty.

The city is home to numerous international organizations, including the United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) and the United Nations Human Settlements Programme (UN-Habitat). Nairobi's real estate market is booming, with modern developments in areas like Westlands, Kilimani, Kileleshwa and Lavington.
